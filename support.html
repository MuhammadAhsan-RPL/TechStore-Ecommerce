You can track your order in several ways:

  • Check your order confirmation email for a tracking link
  • Log into your account and visit "My Orders"
  • Use our Order Tracking Tool with your order number
  • Contact our support team with your order number

Most orders are shipped within 24 hours and tracking information is updated within 2-4 hours of shipment.

International shipping is available to most countries with delivery times of 7-14 business days.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items:

  • 30-Day Return Window: Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Condition: Products must be in original condition with all accessories
  • Refund Processing: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the return
  • Free Returns: We provide free return shipping for defective items

Note: Some items like software, digital downloads, and personalized products may not be eligible for returns.

Setting up your new device is easy! Here's a general guide:

  1. Unbox and inspect: Check all components are included
  2. Charge the device: Fully charge before first use
  3. Power on: Press and hold the power button
  4. Follow on-screen setup: Select language, connect to Wi-Fi
  5. Create/sign in to account: For devices requiring accounts
  6. Install updates: Check for and install any available updates
  7. Install essential apps: Download apps from official stores

For device-specific setup guides, visit our Product Guides section.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and create a new password
  6. Log in with your new password

Tip: Use a strong password with at least 8 characters including numbers and symbols for better security.
